What changed

01
Monochrome, on purpose

Near-black grounds with a warm off-white and one white accent. No hue competes with the app you're dictating into.

02
A capsule, not a card

The resting state is a ~200px capsule: dot, waveform, target app. Live text appears above only while you speak, then disappears. It's a glance.

03
Live text, cheaply

Rolling words from the fast tiny model; the inserted text is re-transcribed at full accuracy on send. Opt-in.

04
AI waits to be asked

Instant verbatim by default. The switch owns Ollama's whole lifecycle with a live status line. No terminal.
